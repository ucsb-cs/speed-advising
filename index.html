Come to the Computer Science Department's Faculty-Undergraduate Advising event. This fun and informational event is offered regularly for Computer Science majors.

Wednesday, Oct 27, 3pm - 6pm on Zoom

These faculty members are available during (part of) this time period. (PDF version) 

The semi-formal title of the event, "Speed Advising," was picked by the student members of the CS Undergraduate Affairs Committee to indicate the flexible 'first-available matchup' format for the meetings between undergraduates and the CS faculty. 

Students will log onto this Zoom link at any time in the above time interval, where they will get some general information about the program and then be matched up with the next available faculty member of their choosing for a 20 minute advising chat. This hopefully provides some positive interactions and  information exchanges between students and faculty members, including a better overview of the research going on in the department, as well as pointers to opportunities, and also satisfies a degree requirement for CS Majors (by getting informed about Major Electives).

Although we won't be able to offer snacks and drinks to participants, we encourage faculty and students to bring their own cup of coffee or tea to enjoy during their conversations.
